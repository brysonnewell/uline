02 // THE PROBLEM

Current Challenges & Business Impact

Current processes rely on email and fragmented folders. This creates tangible risk and delays that are impacting speed-to-market.

Cycle Time Pressure

Reactive prioritization leads to internal pressure and inconsistent turnaround times.

Negative Impact

Slower speed-to-market. Missed vendor start dates.

“There’s a lot of pressure right now... why does it take four weeks to get through our contract?”

Limited Visibility

No shared view into Legal’s queue. Status updates require manual follow-ups.

Negative Impact

Bottlenecks are invisible. Administrative churn chasing status.

“I’m tired of trying to track everybody. I don't want to ask people for updates.”

Fragmented Intake

Requests arrive through email, folders, and ad hoc paths. No single "front door."

Negative Impact

Risk of lost contracts, missed obligations, and audit failure.

“Everyone signs a contract, and they throw it into a drawer... asking ‘who has the contract?’”

The Path Forward

It doesn't have to be this way. DocuSign IAM transforms this chaos into a structured, intelligent system of record—giving you control without the complexity.
