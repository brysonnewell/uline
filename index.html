01 // CONTEXT

Business Context &
Strategic Objectives

Uline operates at an enterprise scale where Procurement and Vendor Management work closely with Legal. As contract volume and complexity increase, the goal is to improve visibility and governance without slowing the business down.

Visibility

02 // CURRENT STATE

Systemic Challenges

Identified challenges within the current ecosystem, representing process gaps rather than team shortcomings.

Fragmented Intake

Requests arrive through email, folders, and ad hoc paths, creating "black holes" for contract status.

“Everyone signs a contract, and they throw it into a drawer... asking ‘who has the contract?’”

Limited Visibility

No shared view into Legal’s queue. Status updates require manual follow-ups.

“What I need is a way to say, look — this is what’s in your legal queue. You can see.”

Cycle Time Pressure

Reactive prioritization leads to internal pressure and inconsistent turnaround times.

“There’s a lot of pressure right now of saying, ‘why does it take four weeks?’”

“The core challenge is operating without a single system of record for contract intake, review, and insight.”

03 // PHASE 1 SCOPE

Getting Started

We are using a "Crawl, Walk, Run" approach. Phase 1 prioritizes adoption and usability before automation.

Agreement Desk

Centralized intake and queues for Procurement and Legal. This replaces the "black hole" of email with a transparent dashboard.

Intake Forms Queues

Navigator

AI-assisted search and centralized repository for executed agreements.

Intake Methods

Intranet URL form ("jacket") + Shared Mailbox integration.

Initial User Group

Focused group (~5 users) to drive adoption.

"I need quick wins."

ServiceNow integration & legacy migration are out of scope for Phase 1.
